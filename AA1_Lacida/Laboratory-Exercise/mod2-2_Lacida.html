One Punch Man

Bald guy saves world by using only one move, his punch. He goes on missions, with his apprentice, to protect his city and defend it from evil. His apprentice, also known as Genos, is a man who suffered trauma and got turned into a cyborg by a scientist. Although he doesn't like working with others, he is forced to work with a team of other powerful humans.
